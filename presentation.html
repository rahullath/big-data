Why 2018 Is The Key
During the 2018 TRY crash, VIX was only 12.5 — global markets were calm. Model A saw calm global indicators: score 24.0 (HEDGE). Model B saw 12 months of lira collapse (TRY/USD: 0.285→0.172): score 40.5 (SPECULATIVE). Different mechanisms. Different data. Different answer.

Academic Finding
A model trained on a global crisis (2009–2011) learns global contagion patterns. It systematically misses country-specific crises driven by political-monetary mechanisms. Minsky's framework requires local institutional data to be actionable.
Practical Implication
Adding TRY/USD lags, VIX, DXY, and Brent to the feature set — all freely available — transforms a model that misses Turkey's biggest crisis into one that detects it 3–6 months in advance.
