Academic Narrative
Model A was trained on a global crisis. It learned to read contagion through global index co-movement. When a Turkey-specific crisis struck in 2018 with global markets calm (VIX=12.5), Model A saw nothing. Model B saw 12 months of lira collapse in its TRY/USD lag features.
