Key Finding
The extended model (2003–2026) successfully detects fragility buildup 8–14 months before each major crisis rupture — events the original model was never trained on.
MAY 2010
Greek Debt Crisis · Flash Crash
Sovereign contagion from Greece drove ISE down 15% in 6 weeks. European counterparty risk spiked sharply. Both models detect this — it was in Model A's training window.
Score 78.4 · PONZI
AUG 2018
Turkish Lira Currency Crisis
TRY/USD lost 40% in weeks on US sanctions and current account stress. Model A scores this as Speculative (43.2) — well below the crisis threshold. Model B correctly flags PONZI (67.8).
Model A: 43.2 · MISSED
MAR 2020
COVID-19 Synchronized Selloff
Global correlation spike: ISE vs SP500 hit r = 0.91 — the highest in the dataset. Both models detect due to global signal strength, but Model B is significantly more accurate.
Score 83.7–88.5 · PONZI
2021 — 2026
Ongoing Turkey Economic Crisis
Unorthodox monetary policy, 80%+ inflation, TRY collapse, ISE nominal gains masking real USD losses. Model A completely misses this (score 38.9). Model B detects persistent fragility elevation (61.4).
Model A: 38.9 · MISSED
